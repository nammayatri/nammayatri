# Project Brief

## Project Goals
- Define the core requirements and goals of the dynamic-offer-driver-app-Allocator project.
- Serve as the source of truth for project scope.

## Core Requirements
- To provide a robust and scalable allocation service for matching drivers to ride requests.
- To manage driver states (e.g., blocking, unblocking).
- To handle financial processes related to drivers (fees, payouts, mandates).
- To ensure driver compliance through document verification retries.
- To send various notifications and alerts to drivers.
- To calculate and manage supply/demand and congestion pricing.
- To integrate with centralized configuration/feature flagging systems (CAC, SuperPosition).
